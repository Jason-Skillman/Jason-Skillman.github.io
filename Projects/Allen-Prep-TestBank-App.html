Allen Prep TestBank App

Android App

Android Developer
Team size: 1

Description:
Allen Prep TestBank is a practice test taking app for the SAT, ACT, IELTS and over 20 other exams. Each exam has its own app variation and around 20 apps/exams are published on the Google Play store.

My Contribution:
Created a native android app that was built from the ground up. The app was thoroughly designed from the beginning to prevent any issues down the road. Created UI wireframes for every page and hooked it up with the back end. The back end consisted of an offline database to store the apps data. Products could be downloaded asynchronously and each product got its own database. I used the strategy pattern to help create and manage various products the company was selling. A Google Play subscription also had to be set up so users could subscribe to a particular set of products.

Challenges:
• Had to use different kinds of REST API’s to download data from the company’s website.
• Managed large amounts of persistent data and stored it in a local SQL database.
• Each product had to be parsed before storing it offline.
• Published and managed each app on the Google Play store for the company.
