Bio

    My name is Jason Skillman and I was born and raised in New Jersey. I attended New England Institute of Technology to study game development and programming. When I was a kid I would make board games and card games out of paper. This would be the start of my career and as I got older I started teaching myself programming and various computer software. By the time I would go off to college I already had a solid programming foundation and I could focus on refining my existing skills while taking on more intense projects. I am currently working as a programmer professionally and am looking for new ways to improve and stand out.
